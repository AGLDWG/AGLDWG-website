Australian Government Linked Data Working Group

Governance

The AGLDWG manages resources which help govern Linked Data resources for Australia, in particular the Internet subdomain linked.data.gov.au which provides persistent identifiers (PID)s.

The group also manages some legacy resources such as the subdomains environment.data.gov.au & reference.data.gov.au which are still providing PIDs.

We also maintain a catalogue of Linked Data resources known to and assisted by the group, see:
