PID Register

This page is the home, or root, page of the linked.data.gov.au main Register.

This Register lists other Register instances, one for each of the classes of things that the Australian govenrment Linked Data Working Group (AGLDWG) issues Persistent Identifiers (PIDs) for. They are:

  • Definitions - ontologies, vocabularies and so on (resource-defining things)
  • Datasets - Linked Data datasets
  • Organisations - AGLDWG organisations
