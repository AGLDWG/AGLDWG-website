Australian Government Linked Data Working Group

Join

Thanks for your interest in joining the Australian Government Linked Data Working Group! We appreciate your enthusiasm for Linked Data and look forward to working more closely with you in coming months. If you would like to join, there are two options:

Stay in touch

If you'd like to receive regular updates about Linked Data in Australia, and have the opportunity to tap into a network of people interested in Linked Data, please subscribe to our mailing list.

 

    

Join the Working Group

If you see yourself as a driver of Linked Data solutions for Australia, if perhaps you'd like to take more of a lead in recommending Linked Data patterns for others, or if you want to promote Linked Data in Australian government, join the Working Group!
